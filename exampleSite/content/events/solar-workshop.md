Join us for an informative afternoon where you'll learn everything you need to know about installing solar panels on your home.

## What You'll Learn

- How solar panels work and what components you need
- Assessing your roof's suitability for solar
- Understanding permits and regulations in Musterstadt
- Financing options and available subsidies
- Choosing the right installer

## Who Should Attend

This workshop is perfect for homeowners who are considering solar but don't know where to start. No technical background required!
