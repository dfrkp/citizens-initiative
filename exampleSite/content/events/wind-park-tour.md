Experience wind energy up close! We're organizing a day trip to the Nordheide Wind Farm, one of the largest in our region.

## Program

- **09:00** - Departure from Town Hall parking lot (carpool)
- **10:30** - Arrival and welcome by the plant manager
- **11:00** - Guided tour including turbine interior visit
- **12:30** - Lunch at local restaurant (self-pay)
- **14:00** - Return journey
- **15:00** - Expected arrival back in Musterstadt

## What to Bring

- Comfortable walking shoes
- Warm clothing (it can be windy!)
- Camera (photos allowed in designated areas)
- Valid ID (required for security)
