## Types of Solar Systems

### Photovoltaic (PV) Systems

PV systems convert sunlight directly into electricity. They're the most common choice for residential installations.

**Advantages:**
- Low maintenance requirements
- 25+ year lifespan
- Can include battery storage
- Feed excess power to the grid

### Solar Thermal Systems

These systems use sunlight to heat water for domestic use or space heating.

**Best for:**
- Households with high hot water demand
- Swimming pool heating
- Supplementing existing heating systems

## Financial Incentives

Several programs support solar installation in our region:

- **Federal subsidy**: Up to 30% of installation costs
- **Low-interest KfW loans**: Special financing for renewable energy
- **Feed-in tariff**: Get paid for electricity you supply to the grid
- **Tax benefits**: Reduced VAT on solar equipment
